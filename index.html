Welcome To ISSS Flappy Bird

How To Play: Press spacebar to start, jump, and restart when its gameover

There will be questions below,

and your aim is to pass through the correct gap.

1 will be at the top, and 2 will be at bottom

A strike will be given if you pass the wrong gap

with no point, 3 strikes and its gameover

If you hit the pipe or the ground, that will be gameover as well.

Have fun! :)
